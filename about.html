About

Architect by training. Product designer by obsession.

The same instinct that drew me to landscape architecture, how do humans actually move through their environments, turned out to be the whole job in product design. I've been obsessed ever since.

The story

From site plans to product surfaces.

I trained as a landscape architect at Clemson, designing how people inhabit space, where the path goes, what gets in the way. After a few years at TBG Partners, I realized the user research side was where my attention went, and I followed it into product.

Smashing Boxes (2019-2020) taught me to ship under client pressure and build design systems that engineers would actually use. Ontada at McKesson (2020-2022) put me inside an oncology EHR, the kind of regulated, high-stakes complexity that teaches you to take craft seriously. ShiftKey (2022-2026) is where leadership and strategy clicked in and I developed a real love for design alignment across an organization.

Now I'm looking for the next place to do that work at scale: a product-led team where design is a first-class voice on the strategy table.
