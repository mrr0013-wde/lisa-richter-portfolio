How I work

Three things I lean on at the senior level.

01, Strategy

Outcomes over outputs.

I start with the business question, what are we actually trying to move?, and reverse-engineer the design surface from there. Roadmaps, north-star vision, and platform thinking are part of the design role, not adjacent to it.

02, Research

Evidence beats opinion.

My approach to research is pretty hands-on and iterative, more like prototyping than theorizing. I'll use whatever method gets us closest to the real signal: surveys, moderated or unmoderated sessions, even quick contextual studies in the field. The goal is simple, learn fast, stay close to what people actually do, and make decisions based on that reality.

03, Craft

Deliverables with thoughtful details.

I focus on designing clear, cohesive experiences without relying on a heavy systems layer. That means creating patterns and components where they add real value, and making sure the details hold up across different touchpoints. I stay close to how designs get built, so what ships feels consistent, intentional, and well-crafted.
